## üéØ Playing the Game

### Tutorial (First Time)
1. **Welcome Screen** - Introduction to trading
2. **Reading Charts** - How to understand price movements
3. **Buying** - When and how to buy
4. **Selling** - When to sell and lock in profits
5. **Portfolio** - Understanding your holdings

üí° **Tip:** Complete the tutorial! It only takes 2 minutes and teaches everything you need.

### Game Interface

**Top Section - Your Stats:**
- **Cash Available:** Money you can use to buy
- **Profit/Loss:** How much you're winning/losing
- **Open Trades:** Number of positions you have

**Middle Section - Price Chart:**
- Simple line chart showing price movement
- üìà Green = Price going UP
- üìâ Red = Price going DOWN
- Tap the chart to see exact prices

**Trading Section - What Would You Like to Do?**
- **Enter Trade** (Green button) - Buy when you think price will go UP
- **Exit Trade** (Red button) - Sell to close and take profits/losses

### How to Trade

**To Enter a Trade (Buy):**
1. Watch the chart
2. When you think price will go UP, tap **"Enter Trade"**
3. You'll get a tip explaining what happens
4. Confirm to buy
5. You now have an "Open Trade"!

**To Exit a Trade (Sell):**
1. When you want to close, tap **"Exit Trade"**
2. You'll see if you made profit or loss
3. Money returns to your Cash Available

**Beginner Tips Appear:**
- Before you buy: "You're buying at $X. You'll make money if price goes UP!"
- After you buy: "Now wait for the price to go UP to make profit!"
- After you sell: Shows exactly how much you made or lost

## ü§ñ Option 3: You vs AI Battle

**Q: Can I pause?**
A: In Simulator mode, yes! Regular game plays in real-time but you control when to trade.
