\begin{abstract}
We present a hierarchical reinforcement learning framework for multi-asset trading that fuses per-asset PPO agents with a SAC meta-controller for portfolio allocation. Our system integrates a Bronze/Silver/Gold medallion data pipeline, multi-source sentiment analysis, conservative Q-learning pretraining, and comprehensive backtesting infrastructure, delivering robust performance across equities, ETFs, commodities, options, forex, and cryptocurrencies.
\end{abstract}
